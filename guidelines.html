Community Guidelines

These rules apply only to Shimmering Community Hub. (Servers May have different rules please refer to your servers deacated rules)

01

General Conduct

  • Follow the platform’s TOS.
  • Keep discussions and interactions civil.
    • "You're stupid for thinking that" "I disagree, here's my perspective..."
  • NSFW content is Not allowed, please reframe for posting it.
  • Usernames and nicknames must be appropriate and not contain profanity, hate symbols, or impersonate staff.
  • Avoid excessive special characters or numbers that make names unreadable.
02

No Spam or Self-Promotion

  • Do not impersonate staff or other community members.
  • Do not share personal data (yours or others’) without explicit consent.
  • Screenshots of private conversations should not be posted without explicit consent.
  • Each channel has a brief description at the top. Post only content that matches the channel’s purpose (e.g., no CMD’s in the #⁠😆general channel).
03

Enforcement

Moderators and admins have the final say in disputes. Follow and respect the instructions of the moderators and administrators.

04

Language

  • Please use English in text channels for easier communication.
  • Avoid excessive profanity. Keep it friendly and suitable for all ages.
05

Voice Channels

  • Keep voice volume at a reasonable level.
  • Be mindful of others when in voice channels. Do not interrupt or talk over people.
06

Recruitment and Self-Promotion

  • Do not spam recruitment messages.
  • Self‑promotion, server invites, or links to external services are only allowed in the ⁠#📡advertise channel.
07

Events and Tournaments

  • Follow any additional event-specific guidelines posted by organizers (e.g., tournaments, role-play settings).
  • Respect the decisions made by event organizers.
08

Bot Usage Policy

  • Only bots approved by the admin team may be added to the server.
  • Users must not misuse bot commands to flood channels or harass others.

Enforcement Actions

We use a progressive discipline system designed to educate and correct behavior while maintaining community safety.

Warning

Verbal or written warning for minor first-time violations

No duration

Timeout

Temporary restriction from sending messages

60 Secs - 1 Week

Server Ban

Removal from SCH

1 Day - Permanent

Network Ban

Removal from SCN Entirely

1 Month - 2 Years

NB - Trust & Safety

The only form of Network Ban that can be Permanent.

Permanent

Violation Severity

Low Severity

  • Minor off-topic discussions
  • Accidental rule violations
  • First-time mistakes

Medium Severity

  • Repeated minor violations
  • Mild inappropriate content
  • Disruptive behavior

High Severity

  • Harassment
  • Threats or violence

Network Bans

Internal Network Bans

Issued for violations occurring within SCN:

  • Coordinated disruption of SCN or server raiding.
  • Harassment Campaigns directed at SCN Members/Staff.
  • Doxing Staff/Members.
  • Impersonation of a SCN staff Member.
  • False accusations made against Staff/Members with malicious intent.
  • Glorification of suicide, self-harm, or violence.
  • Large-scale drama originating from/aimed at SCN.

External Network Bans

Issued only when actions on external platforms/communities directly impact SCN:

  • Actions on external platforms that directly target, threaten, or endanger the community.
  • Doxing Members/Staff.
  • Coordinated harassment campaigns targeting SCN from outside source.

Important Clarification

  • Disagreements or general arguments on platforms/communities unrelated to SCN.
  • Personal actions on external platforms unrelated to community members.
  • Previous actions that have not been shown to have an impact on community safety.

Trust & Safety Network Bans

  • Stalking A.K.A Backdooring.
  • Sexual Harassment.
  • And More That We Can't Talk About on The Internet.

Caution

Trust and Safety Bans are rarely appealed, so the only real way to lose one is to either give us undeniable proof you didn't do it or, the best option, to prove your innocence to a court. That's just the unfortunate truth.

Reporting Issues

We take all reports seriously. Our moderation team reviews every report within 24 hours.

1

Gather Evidence

Take screenshots or note message IDs. Evidence helps us take appropriate action quickly.

2

Use the Report System

Right-click the message > Apps > Wick > and select "Report Message"

3

Provide Details

Explain what happened and why you're reporting. Include relevant context.

4

Wait for Review

Our team will review your report within 24 hours and take appropriate action.
